This interactive map is a supporting output of a study mapping carbon sequestration in smallholder oil palm plantations in Pelalawan Regency, Riau Province, Indonesia, using satellite remote sensing and machine learning. Sub-district values are aggregated directly from mask-constrained zonal statistics, seamlessly combining all administrative polygon fragments and rounded to two decimal places. The regional total (2.67 MtC) reflects the unrounded, scientifically precise aggregate and may differ slightly from the sum of the rounded sub-district figures shown below, a standard rounding discrepancy in spatial statistics. Click any sub-district boundary on the map for a detailed breakdown of its carbon stock.
